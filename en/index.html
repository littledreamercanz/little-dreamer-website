NZ Tax FAQ

Common accounting and tax questions

When do I need to register for GST?

If taxable activity turnover reaches $60,000 over the past 12 months, or is expected to reach $60,000 in the next 12 months, GST registration is generally required.

Can GST returns be monthly, two-monthly or six-monthly?

Yes. The right filing frequency depends on turnover, cash flow, record quality and the IRD registration settings.

What is provisional tax?

If your prior-year residual income tax is more than $5,000, provisional tax instalments may apply in the following year.

Can messy Xero accounts be cleaned up?

Yes. Cleanup often covers chart of accounts, bank reconciliation, GST coding, supplier/customer balances and payroll journals.
