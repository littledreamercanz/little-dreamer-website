# Pawora Pets Ecommerce Preview

Static preview for a NZ/AU pet accessories ecommerce concept.

Features:

- Bright pet-focused hero and category imagery.
- Dog, cat, travel, grooming and aquarium accessory categories.
- Product filtering and search.
- Product detail dialog with color/size selections.
- Cart drawer and simulated warehouse checkout.
- Positioning focused on non-food, non-medicine, low-risk accessories.

This preview can be migrated to Shopify later:

- Products become Shopify products and variants.
- Cart/checkout becomes Shopify checkout.
- Stock status maps to Shopify inventory locations.
- Warehouse flow can connect to a 3PL or manual fulfillment process.
